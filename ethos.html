Ethos

Providing a service that's accessable to all is important to me and I hope you see this reflected in my services.

I care about my impact on the environment which is why I've limited the places that I'm willing to drive to to provide my service. I also aim to be as paper free as possible in the pursuit of my service.

I give a percentage of my tutoring profits to Bedford Food Bank and Emmaus.
